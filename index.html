About me

I am an Ikerbasque Research Fellow and a La Caixa Junior Leader Postdoctoral Fellow in the Singularity Theory and Algebraic Geometry group at BCAM – Basque Center for Applied Mathematics.


My research interest is the study of singularities from the algebraic point of view, especially multiplicity theory and positive characteristic methods in commutative algebra. You can learn more about my research here.
